Introduction:


This article is a reflection of my experience getting my hands dirty with digital forensics for a school assignment that threw me right into the deep end of certain concepts that we briefly skimmed over in class. Before this assignment, my experience with forensics in the terms of cybersecurity came from CTF competitions where I spent an overly absurd abundance of time staring at the challenges in frustrated confusion, or looking at phishing emails and analyzing them to build an understanding on what they contained. This assignment forced me to become intimately familiar with tools and concepts that I have never used / done before, including downloading a raw dataset, extracting it, and analyzing it with a tool called FTK Imager.


Goals Of The Assignment:


The assignmenthad four (4) main goals that I had to accomplish, which were as follows:

  1. Research and understand the story of Enron and the scandal they faced.
  2. Download the Enron dataset and analyze it using FTK Imager.
  3. Find messages that were sent to the company's CEO with words of support or resentment.
  4. Find the company's lobbyist messages related to the Canadian government (Provincial and Federal).

This gave me a clear scope of what I needed to accomplish, and allowed me to break down the assignment and develop a clear plan of attack.
