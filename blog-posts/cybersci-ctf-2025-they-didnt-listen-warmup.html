I started the challenge by running an nmap scan on the provided IP address to see what ports were open.

We were instructed by the competition organizers to ignore any and all open port 22 services, so I turned my attention to port 443 which meant that the address had an HTTPS service running. URL Visited: https://10.0.2.11/robots.txt
