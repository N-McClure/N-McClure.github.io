Introduction:


This week at ISSessions, I presented a talk during our news segment about a bug covered in an article about a bug in a popular WordPress plugin with over 60'000 installations that allows attackers to create admin accounts. I found this article interesting because WordPress is something that I have been experimenting with for most of the year and when critical scoring velnerabilities are found in plugins that are so popular in the wild, it is important to understand the risks of them and share with the public.
